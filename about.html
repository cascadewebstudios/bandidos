Redmond's Favorite Taco Truck

Our Story

Bandido’s was born from a love of bold flavors, street food, and the creative freedom of taco trucks. With a grill, a boom box, and a dream, we began serving food with a twist — birria tacos stuffed with cheese, loaded nachos, and sizzling burritos rolled tight in homemade tortillas. Our crew includes dedicated grill lovers, taco heads, and friends from the neighborhood. At Bandidos, we don’t just make tacos — we serve flavor with an edge.

VOTED BEST FOOD TRUCK ON THE EASTSIDE 2024

At Bandido’s our mission is to serve bold, unforgettable tacos that bring people together and celebrate the spirit of street food with every bite.
